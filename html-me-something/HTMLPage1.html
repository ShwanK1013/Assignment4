Mustang Mach E!

Ford came out with a new electric SUV that is in direct competition with Tesla model Y! It's a very bold move by Ford to make a tech heavy vehicle with reasonable price. Ford Mach E has little lower range than Model Y, However, a lot of EV enthusiasts are excited that there is finally a vehicle that can compete with Tesla!

Mustang Mach E!

Ford finally created a tech heavy car! Although being tech heavy can be a great feature, they are still a step behind Tesla. Ford announced that they will publish a software update for "Hands Free drive", which is a countermeasure of Tesla's "Auto Drive". However, the tech is not out yet, it's planned to be out in the 3rd quarter of 2021. Mach E with extended range battery and RWD can drive up to 305 miles! It's a unbelievable range! Most companies who put their feet into the EV market to compete against Tesla always felt short in range and price. Now that MSRP of Mach E California 1 is just little under 50k USD and has capability to drive up to range of 305, we can finally say that there is a direct competition against Tesla. Mach E is also known for having better built quality. Tesla came to EV market in few years ago, without much of vehicle manufacturer experience, Tesla's been criticized and accepted by consumers that the quality of their car is horrible. Some even say it is a lottery to get a good quality Tesla car. But, Mustang Mach E has been praised to have great built quality compared to Tesla! Although Mach E just came out recently, I am looking forward to see Ford's commitment and competition against Tesla!

Price of Mustnag Mach E

Ford Mach E comes into EV market with base model MSRP of $42,000. Then, it goes up to $47,000 for premium model, $49,000 for California Route 1 model and $59,000 for GT model. I believe Mach E definitely has potential to compete against Tesla Model Y!
